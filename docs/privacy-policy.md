# Privacy Policy

**DrinkWater** does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal user data.

The app stores hydration data, profile settings, language preference, reminder settings, and statistics locally on the user’s device.

DrinkWater does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, external databases, or user accounts.

The app may use local notifications only when the user enables water reminders.
